Team

The Translational Cancer Research Laboratory at Incheon National University studies the molecular mechanisms driving tumor initiation and progression. Our research employs interdisciplinary approaches to investigate microRNA, exosome biology, and metabolic signaling in tumor progression and therapeutic resistance.
